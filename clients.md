01  |  PROJECT PURPOSE & OVERVIEW

Salt Route Consulting Pvt. Ltd. requires the design and development of a vibrant, high-quality property showcase website to represent our managed hospitality and short-term rental properties. The website will serve as our primary digital marketing channel — directing potential guests and property partners to our listings and driving direct enquiries.

This document provides Kurly Brains with the full brief, content structure, design direction, and delivery timeline required to complete Phase 1 of the website.

CLIENT	Salt Route Consulting Pvt. Ltd.
DEVELOPER	Kurly Brains
PROJECT TYPE	Multi-property hospitality showcase website — Phase 1
NO. OF PROPERTIES	3 Properties (Phase 1)
TARGET AUDIENCE	International & domestic travellers, trekkers, expats, corporate guests, property owners
BRAND TONE	Vibrant, premium, warm, trustworthy — Nepal's finest hospitality

02  |  PROPERTIES TO BE FEATURED

The website must prominently showcase the following three properties. Each property must have its own dedicated section or page with a strong visual identity, photo gallery, key features, and a clear call-to-action (enquiry / booking link).

PROPERTY 01  ·  SUNSHINE VILLA ILAM
📍 Ilam, Eastern Nepal
Nepal's green jewel — a premium villa retreat surrounded by Ilam's world-famous tea gardens.
•	Full property photo gallery (Salt Route to supply high-resolution photos)
•	Key features listed: views, rooms, amenities, outdoor spaces
•	Location context: Ilam district, proximity to tea gardens and trekking routes
•	Booking call-to-action button linking to Airbnb / direct enquiry form
•	Short description of the Ilam experience — tea country, fresh air, tranquility

PROPERTY 02  ·  SUNGAVA RESIDENCES
📍 Sungava Phulbari, Pokhara, Gandaki Province
A modern 3BHK apartment 20 minutes from Pokhara's Lakeside — mountain views, fully furnished.
•	Full photo gallery using the images supplied to Kurly Brains
•	Highlight: open-plan teal kitchen, mountain-view bedrooms, modern interiors
•	Location context: Pokhara proximity, Annapurna views, airport distance
•	Airbnb listing link + direct enquiry button
•	Guest capacity, bedroom count, and key amenities clearly displayed

04  |  DESIGN DIRECTION & BRAND GUIDELINES

Visual Style
•	Vibrant, full-bleed photography — properties must look stunning
•	Clean, modern layout — white space, elegant typography
•	Premium feel — not budget accommodation, not generic template
•	Mobile-first responsive design is mandatory
•	Fast load times — photos must be optimised for web

Colour Palette
•	Primary: Deep navy (#1B3A5C) and warm gold (#C9A96E)
•	Accent: Cream and warm beige for backgrounds
•	Use the Salt Route brand colours throughout
•	Property pages may have subtle individual colour accents	Typography
•	Headings: Elegant serif font (e.g. Playfair Display or similar)
•	Body: Clean sans-serif (e.g. Inter or Lato)
•	All text must be legible on both light and dark backgrounds

Functional Requirements
•	Enquiry/contact form on each property page
•	Links to Airbnb and social media listing
•	WhatsApp quick-contact button (floating or in footer)
•	Google Maps embed for each property location
•	Photo gallery with lightbox on all property pages
•	SSL certificate — https:// required
